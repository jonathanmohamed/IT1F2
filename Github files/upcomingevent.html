International Open Day

Would you like to experience what it is like to study in the Netherlands at Arcadia University of Art and Technology?
Meet our academic staff and other (international) students!

Visit one of our International Open Days in Emmen and experience what it is like to study here. During the event you
can learn all about our English-taught programmes and meet staff and students with multi-cultural backgrounds.
The International Open Day is specially organised for all students who are coming from abroad to visit AUAT.
